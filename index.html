About Our App

Intentional is a daily planner and goals tracking app based on the science of behavior and happiness. Living intentionally means deliberate choices about what to include and what to leave out of your day. Our app helps you live in the moment, prioritizing what matters and leaving the noise out.

Take us anywhere

We love to travel with you. Intentional is designed to be accessible from your mobile, tablet, or browser. You will always have access to your daily intentions and your journal with us.

Secure

Intentional is a secure place for your thoughts and priorities. Unlike paper and pen journal, Intentional is only accessible through the app. You control access, and your thoughts stay with you.

Reaseach-based

Our app takes a research-based approach to help you achieve happiness. We weave many disciplines into the app, including behavioral economics and cognitive psychology. Leverage the power of these insights to help you get things done.

Virtual life coach

We are passionate about helping people achieve their goals. The Intentional app is built to help coach you from idea to reality. Along the way, we will teach you strategies to ensure you succeed.

Easy To Customize

Unlike other planners, Intentional is built to change what works for you. Want to journal about gratitude? Do it! Not so much? You can choose to leave that out of your daily stream.

Visualize your success

Intentional is designed to help you find patterns of behavior. Unlike paper journals, Intentional aggregates important data and displays it to you. Have you ever wondered how many tasks you completed last week? Intentional summarizes everything for you in an easy chart.

How The App Works?

Sign-Up

Signing up for intentional is easy. You can link Intentional to many of your existing accounts (Facebook, Apple, Google). Once you create your account, your data is stored safely and can be accessed only by you!

Take your life assessment

Our life assessment will help you understand the balance of your life. We assess your life across 8 categories and then help you understand where you are invested and where you might want to invest.

Set some goals

Once you understand your life's current balance, we help you set goals to create the life you want. These goals are selected using our goal coaching flow. This uses research-based strategies to help you set and achieve your goals. This feature is like nothing else on the market, helping you identify what you want and how to get it.

Start living intentionally

With everything set up, it is time to start living intentionally. Our easily customizable Daily Planner helps you set up your day with clear intentions. The daily planner makes it easy to track your day and reflect on your days to improve your life balance.
