Science based strategies for greater happiness.

We use the latest reseach in human happiness and behavior to help you live a happier life.

Daily Intentions

Our lives are busy and prioritization is difficult. Interntional is designed to help you think through complexity and focus on what matters.

Secure

Unlike pen and paper planners Intentioal is built with privacy as a first principle. Share what you want, and keep private what you want.

Success over time

Looking at your behavior patterns over time is one of the best ways to help reflect on change. Intentioal gives you the tools to understand your productivity, gratitude, and goals over time.

Gratitude

Science show that those who take time to remember all the things that are wonderful in their life are happier. Intentional helps you remember to take time and reflect on how wonderful your life is.

Meet the team

Brett (left) and Nick (right) met in San Francisco in 2018. We bonded over a shared love of technology and always planned to collaborate on on an app.

Fast-forward to 2020; after a long year we both felt like this was the time to work on a project that would help people find inner strength and direction. The Intentional App is designed with love to help you find your way.
